# Product

## Register

product

## Users

VibeSong serves social media users who want a fast, accurate song match for an Instagram or TikTok Story, plus internal operators maintaining the catalog that powers those matches.

## Product Purpose

The product lets a user upload a photo or video, analyzes the visual mood, and returns swipeable song recommendations that feel specific to the moment. Success means users quickly save at least one song and trust that the catalog tags behind the matching are reliable enough to build future requested-vibe features on.

## Brand Personality

Expressive, sharp, music-native. The interface should feel energetic and stylish without getting in the way of the matching workflow.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic playlist-manager UI, stock music-library dashboards, overdecorated AI SaaS layouts, and interfaces that hide operational catalog quality problems behind vague scores.

## Design Principles

1. Make the match feel earned: expose the signals that explain why a song belongs.
2. Keep the workflow fast: upload, review, swipe, save, or fix catalog data with minimal friction.
3. Treat uncertainty as useful data: low confidence should guide review without blocking exploration.
4. Protect trust: never expose API secrets client-side and keep AI work on server routes.
5. Stay music-native: use expressive accents and compact rhythm, but prioritize clear controls over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG AA contrast as the baseline, maintain keyboard-accessible controls, support reduced motion preferences, and avoid relying on color alone for status or confidence.
